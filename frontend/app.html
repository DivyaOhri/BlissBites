What is BlissBites? 🍜

BlissBites is a smart, emotion-aware food recommender built specifically for students at Shoolini University, Solan. It combines affective computing, vector semantic search, and real local menu data to recommend food that actually matches how you feel — not just what's popular.

🧠 Why This Matters

  • People feel confused about what to eat
  • Mood directly affects food preferences
  • Decision fatigue is real and exhausting
  • This app makes the decision for you
  • Familiar comfort food helps with anxiety

🔬 The Technology

  • ChromaDB vector database
  • Sentence-transformers embeddings
  • Google Gemini API for natural language
  • RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)
  • LangChain orchestration framework
  • Streamlit frontend

🌿 Wellness Tips

😰 If stressed

  • Drink warm tea or soup
  • Eat light comfort food
  • Avoid skipping meals

😴 If tired

  • Choose easy-to-digest meals
  • Avoid heavy fried food
  • Stay hydrated

😊 If happy

  • Try new cuisines
  • Eat balanced meals
  • Share food with friends

😟 If anxious

  • Stick to familiar food
  • Avoid too much caffeine
  • Warm food calms the nerves
